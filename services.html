As global economic and political landscapes are rapidly transforming, people must realize how international political and economic issues will directly affect their lives. Alpha Institute for Global Issues is committed to diverse research exploring the trends of social development and dedicated to approaches to making it clear the connection between people and policy . We provide straightforward facts, information, analysis and conclusion of research to inspire people to better prepare to face challenges.

Alpha Institute for Global Issues takes complex issues and ensures they are accessible to everyone through traditional and nontraditional media and we always welcome smart and motivated thinkers, researchers and analysts with a core interest in global economy and politics.
